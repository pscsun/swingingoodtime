“The Nearness of You” (Carmichael) - Duet with Barbara Morrison
 Recorded 13th April 2010 at Jazushi Restaurant, Sydney. Performing Hoagy Carmichael's ballad: "The Nearness of You" as a duet with the fabulous Barbara Morrison. With Monique Lysiak (piano), Alex Compton (bass) & Ian Bloxsom (drums).
With sincere thanks to Barbara for generously sharing her time, voice & enormous talent for this special moment.
