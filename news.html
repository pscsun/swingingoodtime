31/01/13: Things are a‘happening at the ArtHouse Hotel!
Friday Jazz @ The ArtHouse is back!
 2013 sees the fabulous ArtHouse Hotel reintroduce and reinvigorate its Friday night jazz series with a new theme: 'Swing Sophisticated!'. Paul will be performing regularly with his trio in the beautiful Dome Restaurant and Lounge, and is proud to announce that they have been selected to be artists-in-residence for this jazz series. Check out the Gig Guide page for Paul’s performance dates there.
The ArtHouse Hotel Bridal Open Day:               
 Saturday 16th Feb also sees the ArtHouse open its doors to show prospective wedding clients its merits as a unique heritage listed wedding venue. Dedicated wedding planners will be on hand and guided tours will be conducted of the venue's ceremony & reception spaces. The ArtHouse's preferred/trusted suppliers will be on site (including Paul, performing with his trio) to provide you with information and inspiration for your big day! RSVP by registering for a session online to experience a guided tour. Or, simply pop on in to explore the venue!

For any questions contact Alisa (Wedding Specialist) on 02 9284 1200 or aangell@thearthousehotel.com.au. You can find out more about this special event here.
