Paul’s EP: “Swingin’ Good Time!”
Seven cool, smooth & swingin’ tracks for your listening pleasure mark Paul’s recording debut!

Recorded in January 2007 by Dave Price and Rob Wolifson, and featuring Paul (vocals), Brad Madigan (guitar), Tim Brosnan (bass) & Ted Sly (drums), it’s been a long time coming, but we’re sure you’ll agree that it’s been worth the wait!
