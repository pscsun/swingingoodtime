Looking to swing into a Swinging Good Time? Paul Sun is a Sydney-based jazz vocalist who has been likened to jazz greats such as Bobby Darin and Chet Baker and is available to perform at weddings, corporate functions, parties, restaurants, cafés, etc.

 

Performing regularly with a variety of accompaniment, from a solo accompanist to a quintet, Paul's repertoire covers a wide range of popular Jazz standards, from Blues to Latin and, of course, Swing.

 

Please explore this site for more about Paul, including his music and videos of some of his performances, and be sure to visit regularly for further developments.
